# Privacy Policy                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                 
**Vibe Drive**                                                                                                                                                                                 
Last updated: January 28, 2026                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                               
## Overview                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                               
Vibe Drive does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is simple because we don't want your data.                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                               
## Location Data                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                               
Vibe Drive accesses your device location only when you choose to use the "Current Location" feature. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers — we don't have any.      
                                                                                                                                                                                               
## Third-Party Services                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                               
To generate routes and analyze terrain, the app sends geographic coordinates (not personal information) to the following services:                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                               
- **Apple MapKit** — Address geocoding and navigation ([Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))                                                                           
- **OSRM** — Route calculation ([Project OSRM](https://project-osrm.org))                                                                                                                      
- **Overpass API** — Map data queries ([OpenStreetMap](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy))                                                                                    
- **Open-Elevation** — Terrain elevation data ([Open-Elevation](https://open-elevation.com))                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                               
These services receive only the coordinates necessary to compute your route. No personal identifiers are sent.                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                               
## Data Collection                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                               
We collect nothing. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking, no cookies, no accounts, no advertising identifiers.                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                               
## Children's Privacy                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                               
Vibe Drive does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
